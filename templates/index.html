About Us

The National Neonatology Forum - SONIC (Sonography for Optimizing Neonatal Intensive Care) focuses on enhancing neonatal care through advanced sonography techniques. This initiative aims to improve diagnostic accuracy and treatment outcomes for critically ill newborns by integrating state-of-the-art imaging technologies into neonatal intensive care practices. By leveraging ultrasound innovations, SONIC enhances the ability of healthcare providers to monitor and manage complex conditions in neonates, ultimately aiming to optimize care and improve survival rates in this vulnerable population.
